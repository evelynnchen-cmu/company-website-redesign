Where Smiles are Made Fresh Daily

Our goal is to put a fresh twist on a familiar treat, and we accomplish this with our 12+ years of experience in the frozen desserts industry.

All of our products use premium ingredients. For example our Premium Homemade Hard Ice Cream is made with a 15% butterfat ice cream base, our Soft Custard is made with a 10% butterfat custard base, and our Water Ice is fat-free and cholesterol-free! Our additional products where you can also find natural and fresh flavors are Stackers, Ice Shakes, Twisters, milk shakes, sundaes, fresh baked cookies, ice cream sandwiches, ice cream cakes, frozen pies, waffle cones and bottled water.

Our fantastic high-quality products will have loyal customers line up out the door and patiently wait for their favorite treat, because we've become an essential part of their lifestyle. Newcomers will quickly realize what they are missing out on and not only adapt the lifestyle, but internalize our slogan “Smiles made fresh daily!”
